Audits and Controls

  • We do concurrent audits for web-series, Events, Daily Soaps and Movie
  • Presence of Audit team on Set ensure proper recording of Talents, Production Staff, Workers, Equipment‘s, Purchases and Hiring
  • Daily MIS or Daily Productivity Reports ensures all the MIS are accurate and available in Soft form thereby eliminating the need to open daily Dockets
  • Cost reports once or twice a week ensure the producer are well aware of financial boundaries
  • Comparison of Actual cost to Budgets allows producers to have a sense of actual spending and giving a direction to production team
  • Vendor Audits ensures the bills submitted by vendors are duly checked before payments.
  • Also we have ongoing discussion with production, existing vendors and management for all the matter related to cost

Budget Preparation

  • We prepare a detail report with respect every line item required in production of Web-Series or any type of Content. Budget preparation is a crucial activity and ensures financial expectations and limitations are Set first.
  • We also do Budget verification on behalf of producers to ensure accurate target is set for a producer by an investor or financer.

Vendor Negotiations

  • Our Database have wide variety of locations List, equipment material and purchase cost which are used in daily shoots. The database ensure producers do not overpay for purchase or hiring.

Accountancy services for production house

  • Quite often it is observed that smaller production houses produce only one or two web-series a year. It is essential to note that such production house hire accountant for an entire year.
  • Our Accountancy services billings are on project basis and thus the producer pays only till the project is live. Also the experience team of accountants ensures accounting is done on timely basis including compliances like GST filing.
